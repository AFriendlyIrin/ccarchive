Opening

(The chickens stand at the top of a great pyramid overlooking a setting sun.)
vermillion

empress
Beautiful, is it not? From here, you can see much of my empire.
joyful jo
Don't let eagerness take hold.
There is still a harsh path you must walk before you are ready for ascension.
empress
I do not recall extending my invitation to you.
joyful jo
You do not own these sacred grounds for our race.
empress
I am the Great Celestial Jade Empress, why should this not be mine?
Your apprentice should see the Gate for himself before your teachings ruin him.
vermillion

joyful jo
Now we get to the crux of the issue.
empress
Silence. The gate only opens once all the celestial bodies have aligned.
I simply haven't had the opportunity.
(Brightbeak warps in.)
brightbeak
All three heinous villains appear at a single location!
What an auspicious day, for evil shall be eradicated.
empress

brightbeak
Empire? What is an Empire without citizens? An Empress without subjects?
The Crescent has suffered your delusions and tyranny for long enough!
empress
What gives you any right to question my legitimacy, foul creature?
brightbeak
Unlike you, false ruler, I have loyal ascenders who heed my call!
Behold, the warriors of righteousness, the Virtuous Veil!
(An army of chickens warps in.)
joyful jo
An ambush, from a supposed chicken of righteousness.
brightbeak
There is no line that cannot be crossed in pursuit of eradicating evil.
joyful jo
We are simply passing by the Skyward Gate. We have no business with your... coup.
empress

joyful jo
You never invited me, Celestial Highness.
brightbeak
Don't make me laugh.
As long as you pursue your wicked quest of ascension, you are an enemy of the land! I shall end this quest. The Skyward Gate must fall.
empress
To believe it is just to taint sacred ground, you have truly gone mad, Brightbeak!
vermillion
What makes you oppose the noble goal of ascension?
brightbeak
You know nothing, witless hatchling.
The consequences of the last ascension have scourged this realm for centuries!
No longer shall the selfish desires of the few plague our kind as a whole.
vermillion
And what shall you do after the Gate is no more? When there is no purpose to fulfil?
brightbeak
The bloody traditions that tears us apart shall finally crumble.
What greater purpose can there be compared to a life of peace?
vermillion
There is no purpose to living except to surpass living.
To destroy our path to ascension is to end purpose.
brightbeak
Are you so far gone that you do not recognise the value of life itself?
vermillion
Life is a shackle that must be broken.
Enough words. I see you are a raving lunatic indifferent to reason. Begone.
brightbeak
Hah! Virtuous Veil, we shall destroy these three fiends at once! For all that is good!

joyful jo

vermillion

See for yourself what you are.
See that your doubts, your fears, your attachment to the mortal realm, remain.
vermillion
So that is all I need to dispose of to ascend?
vermillion?
You cannot dispose of them. That is you.
vermillion
On the contrary, I can. I'll just tear out the parts of me that drag me down.
vermillion?
That is destruction of ego. It will no longer be you, no longer be us, that ascends.
vermillion
There is no value in life besides to ascend.
(Vermillion cuts out a shade of himself and discards it.)
vermillion
Now, I will ascend.
vermillion?
